Product Strategy Process

In 2023, parts of the product department implemented and tested a new process that led the team to be consistent and mindful, ensuring continuous added value that is verified.

Reduce churn and improve sales

Commercialize our products with clients in mind

We need to close the gap between our products and the market's needs. We will relentlessly concentrate on understanding our customers, and support our marketing and sales strategies. Commercialization is the cornerstone of our journey towards industry leadership.

Increase opportunities

Impact the entire construction-industry

We will not only seek success within our sector but also strive to elevate the entire industry. By engaging with and contributing to the broader community, we will create opportunities for our business and our partners to establish ourselves as thought leaders.

Process led

Create focus towards shared goals

We will champion a process-driven approach that puts objectives at the forefront. By defining clear, common objectives, we will ensure alignment throughout the department, fostering a collective effort to build a path forward towards our shared goals.
